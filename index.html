  • WELCOME TO BRIGHT KITCHEN UTILITIES STORE

    Mr. Bright Hassan owns and operates BRIGHT KITCHEN UTILITIES STORE. It is focused on the manufacturing of culinary tools. It specializes in the design, supply, installation, support, and lifecycle maintenance of commercial kitchen and catering equipment. We've worked with a variety of businesses, including hotels, restaurants, schools, bars, and remote camps, to provide commercial kitchen design and installation services. The company is based in Nyarugenge, Kigali City, and operates out of T2000 House on KG 588. BRIGHT KITCHEN UTILITIES STORE

    BRIGHT KITCHEN UTILITIES STORE offers the greatest kitchen accessories at a reasonable price. Our accessories are the greatest since they have earned numerous prizes for their superior quality. Our accessories include a variety of knives, casseroles, plates, dishes, refrigerators, and a variety of other items.
